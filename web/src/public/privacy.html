Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the Where app (the "App") and the location tracking service it provides (the "Service").

Information We Collect

The Service is designed to be privacy-focused. We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information.

When you enable online tracking, the App sends your location data to our server. This data is identified only by a random, anonymous client ID that you can share with others. We do not associate this ID with any personal information.

We collect crash logs via Firebase Crashlytics to improve app stability.

How We Use Information

The location data is used solely to display your live location to anyone who has your client ID. The data is not stored long-term and is automatically deleted from our servers after a short period of inactivity.

Information Sharing

We do not share your location data or any other information with third parties.

Data Retention

Live location data is temporary. We do not retain historical location data.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us.
